Pitch

What if Tinder was a video game?
From creating a profile and matching with desired partners, to chatting and going on dates. 'Match, Talk, Date! - A modern dating sim!' has it all.

You control the conversation!
MTD has an in-depth dialogue system where it not only matters what you say, but also what you said in the past and a lot of additional factors such as your relationship status, your occupation and bio, your age and gender, your game settings, the current time, and much more!

Description

‘Match, Talk, Date!’ is a modern take on the classic dating sim genre, where you use a dating app to chat to a diverse cast of unique characters and eventually ask them to go out on a date with you! Play how you want and date who you want, both men and women are available!

Can you convince them to go out on a date with you, or are you going home alone?

History

Development started in 2020 during the corona lockdowns as a passion project with the absurd question of "What if your mouse cursor was a life-sized hand?".
While the life-sized hand is still part of the project, an option was later added to replace it by a more normal looking cursor when the game started taking shape in the form of a dating sim.

Since then the focus of the project has been to create a modern take on the classic dating sim genre by using a dating app, where the player is given as much freedom as possible within the conversations.
While most core features of the game are already implemented, there is still a lot of work left to be done, mainly in adding additional content.
Join us on this exciting adventure as we push the boundaries of love in this digital world.

Features

  • Talk to a diverse cast of characters each with their own personality!
  • Date who you want. Men, women, both? There is something for everyone!
  • Extensive conversation system that shapes itself to the player and situation.
  • Create and shape your own characters with the character creation tools.
  • Bonus minigames. Everyone likes minigames right?
  • Customize your virtual phone. Cute stickers and more!
  • Optional nudity! Play how you want.

About Frozen Lemon Games

Frozen Lemon Games is a one-man company that was created as a side project to serve as the face for games created by Vincent Vangeel. With our first commercial game 'Match, Talk, Date!', we aim to bring a refreshing twist on the traditional dating sim genre by introducing modern technology into the mix.
Because we aren't restricted by funding, we can have more creative freedom and explore unconventional gameplay, although sometimes at the cost of adding more development time.
